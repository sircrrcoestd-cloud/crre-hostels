Rules & Discipline
Code of Conduct for Hostel Inmates
Summary of key rules. Students must refer to the official “Hostel Rules” document for complete details.
Safety · Discipline · Respect

Hostel rules are framed to ensure safety, discipline and an academic environment for all inmates. Students are expected to strictly follow all hostel regulations and instructions issued from time to time.

  • 01
    Rooms are allotted only on full payment of the prescribed hostel fee and are normally treated as occupied for one full academic year.
  • 02
    Inmates must maintain discipline, follow study hours, and avoid any behaviour that disturbs fellow students or affects the reputation of the hostel and college.
  • 03
    Attendance will be taken every night by the resident warden; students must be present in the hostel by the notified time and must not leave without permission.
  • 04
    Ragging, harassment, damage to hostel property, consumption of alcohol, smoking or any unlawful activity is strictly prohibited and will invite severe disciplinary action.
  • 05
    Parents/guardians and local guardians should strictly follow visiting hours; guests can stay only in designated guest rooms with prior approval.
  • 06
    All inmates must cooperate with hostel staff, mess committee and wardens in maintaining cleanliness, punctuality and harmonious communal living.
